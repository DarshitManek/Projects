About Accessibilities

Leading Provider of Manual Websites Accessibility Inspections

ACCESSIBILITIES is the leader in manual website accessibility inspections
and remediation services. Since our founding in 2017 as a web
development consultancy, we have partnered with businesses of all sizes,
attorneys, and professional liability insurance agencies to resolve
thousands of website accessibility barriers. Our comprehensive approach
and on-demand experts enable us to provide the context needed to
ensure successful remediation of website accessibility issues. Our manual,
human-powered website accessibility inspection is 2x more accurate than
leading automated solutions. Our team consists of website accessibility
experts, website development and WordPress specialists, and business
consultants who have the knowledge and experience to help you meet
your accessibility goals.

Frequently asked questions

How does this impact my website?

At ACCESSIBILITIES, we understand the importance of website accessibility and
the need to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA). We provide
comprehensive manual website accessibility inspections and remediation
services to ensure that your website complies with the Web Content
Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). Our inspections and remediation services are
designed to help protect your website from the increasing number of website
accessibility lawsuits and provide an accessible experience for users of all
abilities.
